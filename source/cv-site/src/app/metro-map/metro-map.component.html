The first version of the very unique Hungarian Highway Map, for cartography and transportation-lovers. Every Hungarian motorway and expressway with on- and offramps, rest stops, structures, intersections, notable landmarks and more.
To express my gratitude and the joy of using the Redesigned Parisian Metro Map for almost every day, I wanted to create a visual art on the Hungarian Highway System, using a very similar design language defined by RATP, and Constantine Konovalov.
The map is a metro-style map of the entire Hungarian highway network. The schematic design distorts distances but creates a diagram network, which can be easily read.
