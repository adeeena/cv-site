My Journey

Since a very early age, I learned programming and graphic design by assisting at my father's graphic design company. After my university degree, I had the opportunity to move to Paris, then Nice, to excel in a francophone environment. Now, I'm happy to be back in Paris, continuing my journey.

Beyond my professional work, in 2021 I translated The Gender Dysphoria Bible into Hungarian and French. This sparked my passion for community support projects.

I also launched Egy Transz Podcast, sharing personal experiences and resources. In 2023, I created Gender Útikalauz, a wiki-like information hub, opening doors for collaboration with support associations across Hungary.
